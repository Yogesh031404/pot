üìù Create Your Microsoft Form

  1. Go to Microsoft Forms
  2. Click "New Form" and name it "Eco-Pots Student Registration"
  3. Add these questions in order:
    • Full Name (Text - Required)
    • Roll Number (Text - Required)
    • Email Address (Email - Required)
    • Phone Number (Text - Required)
    • Department (Text - Required)
    • Year of Study (Choice - Required)
      • 1st Year
      • 2nd Year
      • 3rd Year
      • 4th Year
    • Selected Material (Choice - Required)
      • Plastic Bottles
      • Ropes & Strings
      • Old Shoes
      • Glass Jars
      • Metal Cans
      • Other Materials
    • Craft Description (Text - Required)

üîó Share Your Form
